We ordinate the different platforms according to their environmental variables (from the DataPre script) and then cluster to control for the environmental context when testing for difference in structure and function close and far from the platforms. Depth: Water depth (in m) MD0: Median grain size of sediment (Phi units). Mud: Silt/clay content of the sediment (percentage by weight of the sub 63 μm fraction of the sediment). OM: Organic content of the sediment (percentage by weight of organic material in the sediment). Distance: Distance to coast (in m) Current: Bottom current

## Descriptor scores
